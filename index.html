SEMINAR

FOG SCREEN

My seminar topic, "FogScreen," is an innovative and intriguing subject that pertains to a unique technology that has applications in various fields, such as entertainment, advertising, and even education. FogScreen is a revolutionary display technology that creates a thin, walk-through fog or mist screen on which images and videos can be projected. The result is a mesmerizing and interactive visual experience where content appears to float in mid-air within the fog. Unlike traditional screens, FogScreen allows people to walk through the display without obstructing the projection.
